    • Activities

    • Rubbish collection

      • Rubbish collection is a vital part of maintaining a clean and healthy living environment.
      It involves the regular removal of waste materials, ensuring they are disposed of properly and do not pose health or environmental risks.
      To participate effectively in rubbish collection, it's important to follow your local waste disposal guidelines.
      Place your trash in designated bins or containers provided by your municipality and make sure to separate recyclables from non-recyclables.
      Properly sealed bags or containers prevent litter and pests.

    • Recycling

      • Recycling waste is a simple yet powerful way for individuals to contribute to
      a more sustainable future. Recycling involves collecting and processing materials like paper,
      glass, plastics, and metals to be used again, reducing the need for new resources and minimizing the impact on the environment.

    • Waste disposal

      • Proper waste disposal is essential for maintaining a clean and healthy environment.
      It involves the responsible and safe disposal of various types of waste materials, including household trash, recyclables, and hazardous waste.
      To contribute to effective waste management, start by separating your waste into different categories: recyclables,
      organic waste, and non-recyclables. Use designated bins or containers for each type.
